CVT(TR690)-284
Oil Pump
CONTINUOUSLY VARIABLE TRANSMISSION
45.Oil Pump
A: REMOVAL
NOTE:
Refer to Drive Pinion Shaft for removal procedures. <Ref. to CVT(TR690)-244, REMOVAL, Drive Pinion
Shaft Assembly.>
B: INSTALLATION
NOTE:
Refer to Drive Pinion Shaft for installation procedures. <Ref. to CVT(TR690)-246, INSTALLATION, Drive
Pinion Shaft Assembly.>
C: INSPECTION
Check the following items.
Check the oil pump for damage and wear.
Rotate the oil pump by hand, and check that it rotates smoothly.
1) Measure the secondary pressure. <Ref. to CVT(TR690)-49, INSPECTION, Secondary Pressure (Line
Pressure) Test.>
2) Remove the oil pan and oil strainer. <Ref. to CVT(TR690)-112, REMOVAL, Control Valve Body.>
3) Check oil strainer for clogging.
When oil strainer has no clogging, replace the oil pump.
