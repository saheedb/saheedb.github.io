PCV Connector
EMISSION CONTROL (AUX. EMISSION CONTROL DEVICES)
14.PCV Connector
A: REMOVAL
1) Remove the collector cover.
(1) Remove the clips (A).
(2) Hold the shaded area (B) by hand and carefully pull the RH side (C) upward.
(3) Hold the shaded area (D) by hand and carefully pull the LH side (E) upward.
2) Collect the refrigerant from A/C system. <Ref. to AC-26, Refrigerant Recovery Procedure.>
3) Disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
4) Drain engine coolant. <Ref. to CO(w/o STI)-13, DRAINING OF ENGINE COOLANT, REPLACEMENT,
Engine Coolant.>
5) Remove the A/C compressor. <Ref. to AC-45, REMOVAL, Compressor.>
6) Disconnect the preheater hose and PCV pipe from the PCV connector, and remove the PCV connector
from the cylinder block LH.
