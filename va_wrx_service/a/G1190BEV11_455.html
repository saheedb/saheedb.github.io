6MT(TY85)-39
Air Breather Hose
MANUAL TRANSMISSION AND DIFFERENTIAL
10.Air Breather Hose
A: REMOVAL
1) Remove the intercooler. <Ref. to IN(STI)-13, REMOVAL, Intercooler.>
2) Remove the air breather hose.
B: INSTALLATION
1) Install the air breather hose.
2) Install the intercooler. <Ref. to IN(STI)-14, INSTALLATION, Intercooler.>
C: INSPECTION
Make sure the hose is not cracked or clogged.
