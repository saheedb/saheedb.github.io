16.Primary Speed Sensor
A: REMOVAL
CAUTION:
Be sure to prevent water or oil from contacting the connector terminal of primary speed sensor. If ad-
hesion occurs, replace with a new part.
1) Disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
2) Remove the intercooler. <Ref. to IN(w/o STI)-38, REMOVAL, Intercooler.>
3) Clean the transmission exterior.
4) Remove the primary speed sensor harness connector.
5) Remove the primary speed sensor and the spacer.
