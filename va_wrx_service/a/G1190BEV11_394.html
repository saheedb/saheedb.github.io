42.Variator Chain
A: REMOVAL
For removal of variator chain, refer to “Primary Pulley and Secondary Pulley”. <Ref. to CVT(TR690)-228, RE-
MOVAL, Primary Pulley and Secondary Pulley.>
B: INSTALLATION
For installation of variator chain, refer to “Primary Pulley and Secondary Pulley”. <Ref. to CVT(TR690)-232,
INSTALLATION, Primary Pulley and Secondary Pulley.>
C: INSPECTION
Check the variator chain for damage and wear.
