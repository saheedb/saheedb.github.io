Air By-pass Valve
INTAKE (INDUCTION)
11.Air By-pass Valve
A: REMOVAL
1) Remove the intake duct No. 2. <Ref. to IN(w/o STI)-25, INTAKE DUCT NO. 2, REMOVAL, Intake Duct.>
2) Remove the air by-pass valve from the intake duct No. 2.
B: INSTALLATION
Install in the reverse order of removal.
Tightening torque:
3 Nm (0.3 kgf-m, 2.2 ft-lb)
C: INSPECTION
1. AIR BY-PASS VALVE
1) Check that the air by-pass valve has no deformation, cracks or other damages.
2) Connect the Mighty Vac to the nipple (A) of the air by-pass valve.
