COOLING
11.Reservoir Tank
A: REMOVAL
1) Disconnect the over flow hose (A) connected to the radiator filler neck from the reservoir tank.
2) Pull out the reservoir tank in the direction of the arrow while pushing the claw (B).
B: INSTALLATION
Install in the reverse order of removal.
C: INSPECTION
1) Check that the reservoir tank does not have deformation, cracks or damage.
2) Make sure the over flow hoses are not cracked, damaged or loose.
3) Make sure the engine coolant level is between “FULL” and “LOW”.
