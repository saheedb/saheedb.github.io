15.Secondary Speed Sensor
A: REMOVAL
CAUTION:
Be sure to prevent water or oil from contacting the connector terminal of secondary speed sensor. If
adhesion occurs, replace with a new part.
1) Remove the transmission from the vehicle. <Ref. to CVT(TR690)-56, REMOVAL, Automatic Transmission
Assembly.>
2) Remove the transmission cover on the transmission upper side.
3) Remove the secondary speed sensor harness connector.
4) Remove the secondary speed sensor.
