8. Air Breather Hose
A: REMOVAL
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
2) Remove the collector cover.
(1) Remove the clips (A).
(2) Hold the shaded area (B) by hand and carefully pull the RH side (C) upward.
(3) Hold the shaded area (D) by hand and carefully pull the LH side (E) upward.
3) Remove the intercooler. <Ref. to IN(w/o STI)-38, REMOVAL, Intercooler.>
4) Remove the air breather hose.

B: INSTALLATION
Install in the reverse order of removal.
NOTE:
• When installing the air breather hose, pay attention to the positions of the two markings on the hose and
make sure the markings come to the same position as when removed.
• Securely insert the air breather hose until the hose end contacts the catch of the pitching stopper bracket.
C: INSPECTION
Check the hose for peeling, crack or clogging.
