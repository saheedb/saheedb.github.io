5. AT Shift Lock Solenoid and P Range Switch
A: REMOVAL
1. SOLENOID UNIT
1) Remove the AT select lever. <Ref. to CS-24, REMOVAL, Select Lever.>
2) Remove the spacer and gasket. <Ref. to CS-31, DISASSEMBLY, Select Lever.>
3) Using a flat tip screwdriver with a thin tip, remove the harness connector.
4) Raise the claw with a flat tip screwdriver with a thin tip and remove the solenoid unit.
