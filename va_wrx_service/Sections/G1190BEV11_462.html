6MT(TY85)-56
Center Differential
MANUAL TRANSMISSION AND DIFFERENTIAL
17.Center Differential
A: REMOVAL
1) Remove the manual transmission case from the
vehicle. <Ref. to 6MT(TY85)-31, REMOVAL, Man-
ual Transmission Assembly.>
2) Prepare the transmission for overhaul. <Ref. to
6MT(TY85)-38, Preparation for Overhaul.>
3) Remove the extension case. <Ref. to
6MT(TY85)-42, REMOVAL, Extension Case.>
4) Remove the transfer driven gear. <Ref. to
6MT(TY85)-54, REMOVAL, Transfer Driven
Gear.>
5) Disconnect the center differential connector.
6) Remove the adjusting washer and center differ-
ential.
7) Remove the needle bearing.
B: INSTALLATION
1) Install the needle bearing.
CAUTION:
Apply transmission gear oil onto sliding or re-
volving surfaces before installation.
2) Install the adjusting washer and center differen-
tial.
3) When replacing the center differential, select
and install the appropriate transfer drive gear and
adjusting washer. <Ref. to 6MT(TY85)-44, AD-
JUSTMENT, Extension Case.>
4) Connect the center differential connector, and
affix to the oil guide.
5) Install the transfer driven gear. <Ref. to
6MT(TY85)-54, INSTALLATION, Transfer Driven
Gear.>
6) Install the extension case. <Ref. to 6MT(TY85)-
42, INSTALLATION, Extension Case.>
7) Install the manual transmission case assembly
to the vehicle. <Ref. to 6MT(TY85)-33, INSTALLA-
TION, Manual Transmission Assembly.>
C: INSPECTION
Check that there is no damage on the center differ-
ential. Replace if damaged.
