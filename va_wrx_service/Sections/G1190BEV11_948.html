EXTERIOR/INTERIOR TRIM
7. Front Grille
A: REMOVAL
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the bumper face - front. <Ref. to EI-30, REMOVAL, Front Bumper.>
3) Remove the front grille assembly.
(1) Remove the screws on the back side.
(2) Release the claws, and remove the front grille assembly.
CAUTION:
Remove the front grille assembly horizontally. Otherwise, the bumper may be damaged.

B: INSTALLATION
CAUTION:
• Install so that the front end of the under cover (b) comes inside the bumper face - front (a), and the
front end of the mud guard (c) comes outside the bumper face - front (a).
• Before installing the bumper face, match the claws on the bracket - front bumper with the engaging
position of flange section on the bumper face side. If the engaging position is not correct, the flange
section may be broken or the clearance between fender panel and bumper face may not be uniform.
1) Install the front grille assembly.
CAUTION:
When replacing the bumper, be sure to install the spacer grille front to the bumper.
2) Install the bumper face - front. <Ref. to EI-34, INSTALLATION, Front Bumper.>
3) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
4) Adjust the fog light beam. (Model with fog light) <Ref. to LI-43, FOG LIGHT AIMING, ADJUSTMENT, Front
Fog Light Assembly.>
