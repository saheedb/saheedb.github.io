FU(STI)-57
Main Relay
FUEL INJECTION (FUEL SYSTEMS)
20.Main Relay
A: REMOVAL
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery.
2) Remove the glove box. <Ref. to EI-63, REMOV-
AL, Glove Box.>
3) Remove the main relay from relay block.
B: INSTALLATION
Install in the reverse order of removal.
C: INSPECTION
1) Check that the main relay has no deformation,
cracks or other damages.
2) Measure the resistance between main relay ter-
minals.
3) Connect battery positive terminal to terminal No.
3 and battery ground terminal to terminal No. 4, and
measure the resistance between the main relay ter-
minals.
Terminal No. Standard
1 and 2 1 M or more
3 and 4 130.4 230.8 (when 20C (68F))
