6. Front Regulator and Motor Assembly
A: REMOVAL
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery and wait for at least 60 seconds before starting work. <Ref. to
NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the trim panel - front door. <Ref. to EI-56, FRONT DOOR, REMOVAL, Door Trim.>
3) Remove the sealing cover - front door. <Ref. to EB-32, REMOVAL, Front Sealing Cover.>
4) Remove the glass assembly - front door. <Ref. to GW-19, REMOVAL, Front Door Glass.>
5) Remove the regulator and motor assembly - front.
(1) Disconnect the motor - front connector.
(2) Remove the bolts, and then remove the regulator and motor assembly - front.
B: INSTALLATION
1) Install the regulator and motor assembly - front.
Tightening torque:
7.5 N·m (0.8 kgf-m, 5.5 ft-lb)
2) Install the glass assembly - front door. <Ref. to GW-22, INSTALLATION, Front Door Glass.>
3) Install the sealing cover - front door.
4) Install the trim panel - front door.
5) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
