5. Horn Switch
A: NOTE
Horn switch is a unit with the drivers airbag module. For operation procedures, refer to Drivers Airbag Mod-
ule in AIRBAG SYSTEM. <Ref. to AB-33, Drivers Airbag Module.>
B: INSPECTION
1. CHECK HORN SWITCH
1) Remove the drivers airbag module. <Ref. to AB-33, REMOVAL, Drivers Airbag Module.>
2) Check that the horn switch connector is connected properly.
3) Measure the resistance between connectors.
Preparation tool:
Circuit tester
4) Replace the drivers airbag module if the inspection result is not within the standard value.
(a) Ground terminal (b) Horn switch terminal
