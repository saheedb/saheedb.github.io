Fuel Pump Control Unit
FUEL INJECTION (FUEL SYSTEMS)
31.Fuel Pump Control Unit
A: REMOVAL
1) Disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
2) Remove the right side trunk side trim. <Ref. to EI-95, REMOVAL, Trunk Room Trim.>
3) Disconnect the connector (A), and remove the fuel pump control unit.
B: INSTALLATION
Install in the reverse order of removal.
Tightening torque:
5 Nm (0.5 kgf-m, 3.7 ft-lb)
C: INSPECTION
Check that the fuel pump control unit has no deformation, cracks or other damages.
