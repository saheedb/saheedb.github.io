CVT(TR690)-136
Air Breather Hose
CONTINUOUSLY VARIABLE TRANSMISSION
24.Air Breather Hose
A: REMOVAL
1) Remove the intercooler. <Ref. to IN(w/o STI)-38, REMOVAL, Intercooler.>
2) Remove the two air breather hoses.
3) Remove the air breather cap.
4) Remove the oil cap.
B: INSTALLATION
Install in the reverse order of removal.
NOTE:
Use new O-rings.
C: INSPECTION
Check the hose for peeling, crack or clogging.
Check the oil cap is not cracked or clogged.
Check O-ring of oil cap is not damaged.
