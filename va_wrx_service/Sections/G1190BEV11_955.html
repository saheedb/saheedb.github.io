14.Rear Spoiler
A: REMOVAL
1) Remove the trim panel - trunk lid. <Ref. to EI-95, REMOVAL, Trunk Room Trim.>
2) Detach the rear spoiler assembly.
CAUTION:
When removing the nut, be careful not to drop the nut inside the trunk lid panel.
Be careful not to damage the vehicle body during removal and installation.
For model with the large rear spoiler, the reactive force of trunk lid increases when the rear spoiler
is removed.
(1) Remove the plugs (a). (Model with large rear spoiler)
(2) Remove the nuts.
(3) Disengage the clips and remove the rear spoiler assembly.

B: INSTALLATION
1) Clean the trunk lid and spoiler attachment surface before installation.
CAUTION:
Make sure that there is a gasket attached on the clip of the rear spoiler assembly. If a gasket is not
attached, water leakage may occur.
2) Install the rear spoiler assembly.
Tightening torque:
7.5 Nm (0.8 kgf-m, 5.5 ft-lb)
