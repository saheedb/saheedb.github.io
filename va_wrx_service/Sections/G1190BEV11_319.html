SC(w/o STI)-56
Battery Sensor
STARTING/CHARGING SYSTEMS
5. Battery Sensor
A: REMOVAL
1) Disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
2) Disconnect the connector (A) from the battery sensor, and remove the battery sensor.
B: INSTALLATION
Install in the reverse order of removal.
NOTE:
Install the battery sensor to the position where removal/installation of the vent plug is not interfered.
Tightening torque:
6 N·m (0.6 kgf-m, 4.4 ft-lb)
C: INSPECTION
Check the battery sensor for deformation, cracks and any other damage.
