23.Front Fog Light Bulb
A: REMOVAL
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the clips, and turn over the mud guard - front.
3) Disconnect the connector and remove the front fog light bulb. <Ref. to LI-42, DISASSEMBLY, Front Fog
Light Assembly.>

B: INSTALLATION
CAUTION:
Install the mud guard - front so that the front end of the mud guard (b) comes outside the bumper face -
front (a).
CAUTION:
After connecting the connector, make sure that the bulb is locked securely.
1) Install the front fog light bulb. <Ref. to LI-42, ASSEMBLY, Front Fog Light Assembly.>
2) Install the mud guard - front.
3) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
