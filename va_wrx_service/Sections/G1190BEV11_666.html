Control Panel
HVAC SYSTEM (HEATER, VENTILATOR AND A/C)
13.Control Panel
A: REMOVAL
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the panel center assembly. <Ref. to EI-66, REMOVAL, Center Console.>
3) Remove the heater control assembly.
(1) Remove the screws.
(2) Release the claws, and remove the heater control assembly.
B: INSTALLATION
1) Install the heater control assembly.
2) Install the panel center assembly.
3) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
C: INSPECTION
1) Check the illumination operation when battery voltage is applied between the connector terminals.
2) If it does not operate normally, replace the heater control assembly.
