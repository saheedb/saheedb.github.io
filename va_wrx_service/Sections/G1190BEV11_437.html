MANUAL TRANSMISSION AND DIFFERENTIAL
13.Center Differential
A: REMOVAL
1) Remove the manual transmission assembly from the vehicle. <Ref. to 6MT(TY75)-25, REMOVAL, Manual
Transmission Assembly.>
2) Remove the back-up light switch and the neutral position switch. <Ref. to 6MT(TY75)-45, REMOVAL,
Switches and Harness.>
3) Remove the transfer case together with the extension case assembly. <Ref. to 6MT(TY75)-51, REMOV-
AL, Transfer Case and Extension Case Assembly.>
4) Remove the extension case assembly. <Ref. to 6MT(TY75)-51, REMOVAL, Transfer Case and Extension
Case Assembly.>
5) Remove the center differential and transfer driven gear as a unit. <Ref. to 6MT(TY75)-65, REMOVAL,
Transfer Driven Gear.>
B: INSTALLATION
1) Install the center differential and transfer driven gear into the transfer case as a unit. <Ref. to 6MT(TY75)-
66, INSTALLATION, Transfer Driven Gear.>
2) Select the adjusting washer. <Ref. to 6MT(TY75)-53, INSTALLATION, Transfer Case and Extension Case
Assembly.>
3) Install the transfer driven gear. <Ref. to 6MT(TY75)-66, INSTALLATION, Transfer Driven Gear.>
4) Install the extension case assembly. <Ref. to 6MT(TY75)-53, INSTALLATION, Transfer Case and Exten-
sion Case Assembly.>
5) Install the transfer case together with the extension case assembly. <Ref. to 6MT(TY75)-53, INSTALLA-
TION, Transfer Case and Extension Case Assembly.>
6) Install the back-up light switch and the neutral position switch. <Ref. to 6MT(TY75)-46, INSTALLATION,
Switches and Harness.>
7) Install the manual transmission assembly to the vehicle. <Ref. to 6MT(TY75)-30, INSTALLATION, Manual
Transmission Assembly.>
C: DISASSEMBLY
Remove the ball bearing using ST.
NOTE:
Center differential is a non-disassembled part which should not be disassembled.

D: ASSEMBLY
Install the ball bearings.
CAUTION:
Do not apply a load in excess of 10 kN (1 ton, 1.1 US ton, 1.0 Imp ton).
NOTE:
Use a new ball bearing.
E: INSPECTION
1) Ball bearing
Replace the ball bearing in following cases.
• In case of broken or rusty ball bearings
• In case of worn or damaged ball bearings
• When the ball bearings fail to turn smoothly or emit noise in rotation after gear oil lubrication.
• When the ball bearing has other defects.
2) Center differential
If there is wear or damage, replace the center differential assembly.
