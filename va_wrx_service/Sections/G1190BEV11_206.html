26.Main Relay
A: REMOVAL
1) Disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
2) Remove the glove box. <Ref. to EI-63, REMOVAL, Glove Box.>
3) Remove the main relay.
B: INSTALLATION
Install in the reverse order of removal.
C: INSPECTION
1) Check that the main relay has no deformation, cracks or other damages.
2) Measure the resistance between main relay terminals.
Terminal No. Standard
1 and 2 1 MΩ or more
3 and 4 130.4 — 230.8 Ω (when 20°C (68°F))
