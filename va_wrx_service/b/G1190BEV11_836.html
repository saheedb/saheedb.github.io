18.AUX Input Terminal
A: REMOVAL
1. AUX UNIT
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the console box assembly. <Ref. to EI-67, REMOVAL, Console Box.>
3) Release the claws, and then remove the unit - AUX terminal.
2. AUX FEEDER CORD
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
On CVT models, shift the select lever into D before disconnecting the battery ground cable.
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the console box. <Ref. to EI-67, REMOVAL, Console Box.>
3) Remove the panel center assembly. <Ref. to EI-66, REMOVAL, Center Console.>
4) Remove the audio assembly or navigation assembly. <Ref. to ET-8, REMOVAL, Audio.>

5) Remove the clamps and remove the cord assembly - AUX feeder.
B: INSTALLATION
1. AUX UNIT
1) Install the unit - AUX terminal.
2) Install the console box assembly.
3) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
2. AUX FEEDER CORD
CAUTION:
After installing the panel center assembly, check that the air vent grille of the panel center assembly
is inserted correctly into the air vent duct.
1) Install the cord assembly - AUX feeder.
2) Install the audio assembly or navigation assembly.
3) Install the panel center assembly.
4) Install the console box.
5) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
