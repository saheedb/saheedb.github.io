ET-12
Woofer
ENTERTAINMENT
8.Woofer
A: REMOVAL
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the rear seat cushion assembly. <Ref. to SE-29, REMOVAL, Rear Seat.>
3) Remove the trim panel - rear pillar UPR and the trim panel - rear pillar LWR. <Ref. to EI-89, REMOVAL,
Rear Quarter Trim.>
4) Remove the trim panel assembly - rear shelf. <Ref. to EI-92, REMOVAL, Rear Shelf Trim.>
5) Remove the speaker assembly - woofer.
(1) Disconnect the connector.
(2) Remove the bolts and remove the speaker assembly - woofer.
B: INSTALLATION
1) Install the speaker assembly - woofer.
Tightening torque:
7.5 N·m (0.8 kgf-m, 5.5 ft-lb)
2) Install the trim panel assembly - rear shelf.
3) Install the trim panel - rear pillar UPR and the trim panel - rear pillar LWR.
4) Install the rear seat cushion assembly.
5) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
