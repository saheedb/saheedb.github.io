AC-94
Heater Vent Duct
HVAC SYSTEM (HEATER, VENTILATOR AND A/C)
30.Heater Vent Duct
A: REMOVAL
CAUTION:
Before handling the airbag module, refer to CAUTION of General Description in AIRBAG SYS-
TEM. <Ref. to AB-9, CAUTION, General Description.>
1) Disconnect the battery ground cable and wait for at least 60 seconds before starting work. <Ref. to NT-5,
BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the instrument panel assembly. <Ref. to EI-69, REMOVAL, Instrument Panel Assembly.>
3) Remove the screws and remove the duct - center vent (A) and the duct - side ventilation (B) on LH and RH
sides.
B: INSTALLATION
CAUTION:
Before handling the airbag module, refer to CAUTION of General Description in AIRBAG SYS-
TEM. <Ref. to AB-9, CAUTION, General Description.>
1) Install the heater vent duct.
2) Install the instrument panel assembly. <Ref. to EI-77, INSTALLATION, Instrument Panel Assembly.>
3) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
