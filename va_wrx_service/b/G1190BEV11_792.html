37.Ignition Switch Illumination
A: REMOVAL
NOTE:
The ignition switch illumination is integrated into the immobilizer antenna assembly.
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the cover assembly - column.
(1) Remove the screws by turning the steering wheel to right and left.
(2) Release the clips, and remove the cover assembly - steering UPR.
(3) Release the claw, and remove the cover assembly - column UPR and the cover assembly - column
LWR.

For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
