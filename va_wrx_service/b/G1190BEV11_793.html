38.Auto Headlight Beam Leveler Control Module
A: REMOVAL
CAUTION:
Before handling the airbag module, refer to “CAUTION” of “General Description” in “AIRBAG SYS-
TEM”. <Ref. to AB-9, CAUTION, General Description.>
1) Disconnect the battery ground cable and wait for at least 60 seconds before starting work. <Ref. to NT-5,
BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the instrument panel lower cover. <Ref. to EI-61, REMOVAL, Instrument Panel Lower Cover.>
3) Remove the knee airbag module. <Ref. to AB-35, REMOVAL, Knee Airbag Module.>
4) Release the lock and remove the fuse holder.
5) Remove the relay & fuse box.
(1) Disconnect the connector.
(2) Remove the bolts and nuts, and remove the relay & fuse box.

B: INSTALLATION
CAUTION:
Before handling the airbag module, refer to “CAUTION” of “General Description” in “AIRBAG SYS-
TEM”. <Ref. to AB-9, CAUTION, General Description.>
1) Install the auto headlight beam leveler CM.
2) Install the relay & fuse box.
Tightening torque:
7.5 N·m (0.8 kgf-m, 5.5 ft-lb)
3) Install the fuse holder.
4) Install the knee airbag module.
Tightening torque:
7.5 N·m (0.8 kgf-m, 5.5 ft-lb)
5) Install the instrument panel lower cover.
6) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
7) Perform initialization and reinitialization of the auto headlight beam leveler system. <Ref. to LI-16, PRO-
CEDURE, Auto Headlight Beam Leveler System.>
