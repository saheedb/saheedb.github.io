EXTERIOR/INTERIOR TRIM
28.Sun Visor
A: REMOVAL
1) Remove the sun visor assembly.
(1) Remove the cap - sun visor, and then remove the screws.
(2) Disconnect the connector and remove the sun visor assembly.
2) Remove the hook - sun visor.
(1) Press both sides using a flat tip screwdriver.
(2) Remove the hook - sun visor by pulling it toward you.
B: INSTALLATION
1) Install the hook - sun visor.
2) Install the sun visor assembly.
