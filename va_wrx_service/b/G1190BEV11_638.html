Reservoir Tank
POWER ASSISTED SYSTEM (POWER STEERING)
10.Reservoir Tank
A: REMOVAL
1) Drain the power steering fluid.
2) Disconnect the hose - suction and the hose - return.
CAUTION:
Plug the ends of the hose and pipe to prevent foreign matter from entering.
3) Remove the bolt and remove the reservoir - tank assembly.
B: INSTALLATION
1) Install the reservoir - tank assembly.
Tightening torque:
13 Nm (1.3 kgf-m, 9.6 ft-lb)
2) Connect the hose - suction and the hose - return to the reservoir - tank assembly.
3) Replenish power steering fluid up to the specified level. <Ref. to PS-83, INSPECTION, Power Steering
Fluid.>
C: INSPECTION
Check the reservoir - tank assembly for cracks, breakage or damage. If a failure is found, replace the reser-
voir - tank assembly.
