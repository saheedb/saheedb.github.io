Antenna
ENTERTAINMENT
10.Antenna
A: REMOVAL
1. ANTENNA
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery and wait for at least 60 seconds before starting work. <Ref. to
NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Lower the trim panel - roof assembly. <Ref. to EI-99, REMOVAL, Roof Trim.>
NOTE:
Procedures for the trim panel - roof assembly is performed inside the vehicle. Therefore, remove the follow-
ing parts.
Rear seat cushion assembly
Lower inner trim
Upper inner trim
Trim panel - rear pillar UPR
Light assembly - map
Sun visor ASSY
Light assembly - room
Assist rail
Garnish - roof (model with sunroof)
Trim panel - roof assembly
3) Remove the antenna.
(1) Disconnect the connector and release the connector clip.
(2) Remove the nut and detach the antenna.
2. ANTENNA FEEDER CORD
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery and wait for at least 60 seconds before starting work. <Ref. to
NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Lower the trim panel - roof assembly in the same manner as the removal procedure of antenna.
3) Remove the cord assembly - antenna feeder.

ET-15
Antenna
ENTERTAINMENT
B: INSTALLATION
1. ANTENNA
1) Install the antenna.
Tightening torque:
4.5 Nm (0.5 kgf-m, 3.3 ft-lb)
2) Install the trim panel - roof assembly. <Ref. to EI-102, INSTALLATION, Roof Trim.>
3) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
2. ANTENNA FEEDER CORD
1) Install the cord assembly - antenna feeder and the trim panel - roof assembly. <Ref. to EI-102, INSTAL-
LATION, Roof Trim.>
2) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
