Vanity Mirror Light
LIGHTING SYSTEM
36.Vanity Mirror Light
A: REMOVAL
NOTE:
The vanity mirror light is integrated into the sun visor assembly. Replace the sun visor assembly if faulty.
<Ref. to EI-97, REMOVAL, Sun Visor.>
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Release the claws and remove the lens and vanity mirror light bulb.
CAUTION:
• Use a dry clean cloth so that no grease or water adheres to the glass portion of the bulb.
• When using a flat tip screwdriver, apply protective tape or cloth and be careful not to cause dam-
age.
B: INSTALLATION
1) Install the vanity mirror light bulb and lens.
CAUTION:
Use a dry clean cloth so that no grease or water adheres to the glass portion of the bulb.
2) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
