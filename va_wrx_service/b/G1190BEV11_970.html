EXTERIOR/INTERIOR TRIM
29.Assist Grip
A: REMOVAL
1) Pull both sides of the cap (a) using a flat tip screwdriver or similar tool wrapped with a protection tape.
2) Pull the assist grip toward yourself (toward the vehicle center) to remove.
B: INSTALLATION
1) Attach the assist grip.
2) Press in the cap.
