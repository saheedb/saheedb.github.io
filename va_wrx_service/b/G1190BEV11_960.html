1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the pocket assembly.
(1) Attach the protective tape (a) to the panel center LWR.
(2) Remove the damper COMPL - pocket.
(3) Release the stoppers and remove the pocket assembly by pulling it toward you.

B: INSTALLATION
1) Connect the glove box light LED connector.
2) Install the back panel - pocket.
3) Install the ornament - panel assembly passenger.
4) Install the under cover assembly - passenger.
5) Install the pocket assembly.
6) Install the cover - instrument panel side RH.

For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
