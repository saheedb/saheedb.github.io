Keyless Entry Control Module
SECURITY AND LOCKS
22.Keyless Entry Control Module
A: REMOVAL
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the trim panel assembly - rear shelf. <Ref. to EI-92, REMOVAL, Rear Shelf Trim.>
3) Remove the keyless entry control module.
(1) Disconnect the connector.
(2) Remove the bolt and then remove the keyless entry control module.
NOTE:
For models with power amplifier, installation position of the keyless entry control module is different.
B: INSTALLATION
1) Install the keyless entry control module.
Tightening torque:
13 N·m (1.3 kgf-m, 9.6 ft-lb)
2) Install the trim panel assembly - rear shelf.
3) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
(A) Model with power amplifier (B) Model without power amplifier
