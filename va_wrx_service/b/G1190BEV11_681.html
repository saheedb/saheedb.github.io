28.Air Vent Grille
A: REMOVAL
1. CENTER GRILLE ASSEMBLY
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the panel center assembly. <Ref. to EI-66, REMOVAL, Center Console.>
3) Release the claws, and then remove the grille assembly - CTR ventilation.
2. SIDE GRILLE ASSEMBLY
Release the claws, and then remove the grille assembly - ventilation.

B: INSTALLATION
1. CENTER GRILLE ASSEMBLY
CAUTION:
After installing the panel center assembly or grille assembly - ventilation, check that the air vent grille
is inserted correctly into the air vent duct.
1) Install the grille assembly - CTR ventilation.
2) Install the panel center assembly.
3) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
2. SIDE GRILLE ASSEMBLY
CAUTION:
After installing the panel center assembly or grille assembly - ventilation, check that the air vent grille
is inserted correctly into the air vent duct.
Install the grille assembly - ventilation.
C: INSPECTION
1) Check that the direction and amount of air can be adjusted smoothly. Replace the grille assembly - venti-
lation if faulty.
2) Check that the adjustment can be maintained in each position. Replace the grille assembly - ventilation if
faulty.
