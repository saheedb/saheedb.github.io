LI-29
Headlight Bulb
LIGHTING SYSTEM
15.Headlight Bulb
A: REMOVAL
1. HIGH BEAM
CAUTION:
Do not perform work with wet hands, because there is a possibility of electrical shock.
Do not leave the light assembly - head without the bulb for a long time. Dust, moisture, etc. entering
the light may affect its performance.
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the clips and remove the air intake duct. (When removing the high beam bulb RH)
Except for STI model: <Ref. to IN(w/o STI)-22, REMOVAL, Air Intake Duct.>
STI model: <Ref. to IN(STI)-11, REMOVAL, Air Intake Duct.>
3) Disconnect the connector and remove the high beam bulb. <Ref. to LI-25, HIGH BEAM BULB, DISAS-
SEMBLY, Headlight Assembly.>
2. LOW BEAM
CAUTION:
Do not perform work with wet hands, because there is a possibility of electrical shock.
Do not leave the light assembly - head without the bulb for a long time. Dust, moisture, etc. entering
the light may affect its performance.
NOTE:
Since the LED type is a non-disassembled part, it cannot be removed.
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Turn the steering wheel in the opposite direction from the parts to be removed. Then remove the screws
and clips, and turn over the mud guard - front.
3) Disconnect the connector and remove the low beam bulb. <Ref. to LI-25, LOW BEAM BULB, DISASSEM-
BLY, Headlight Assembly.>

Headlight Bulb
LIGHTING SYSTEM
B: INSTALLATION
1. HIGH BEAM
1) Install the high beam bulb. <Ref. to LI-26, HIGH BEAM BULB, ASSEMBLY, Headlight Assembly.>
2) Install the air intake duct. (When the high beam bulb RH was removed)
3) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
2. LOW BEAM
1) Install the low beam bulb. <Ref. to LI-26, LOW BEAM BULB, ASSEMBLY, Headlight Assembly.>
2) Install the mud guard - front.
3) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
