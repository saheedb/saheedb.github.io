28.Immobilizer Antenna
A: REMOVAL
1. MODEL WITHOUT KEYLESS ACCESS
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery and wait for at least 60 seconds before starting work. <Ref. to
NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the cover assembly - column.
(1) Remove the clips, and remove the cover - column.
(2) Remove the screws by turning the steering wheel to right and left.
(3) Release the claw, and remove the cover assembly - column UPR and the cover assembly - column
LWR.

2. MODEL WITH KEYLESS ACCESS
NOTE:
Immobilizer antenna is integrated with the push button ignition switch.
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery and wait for at least 60 seconds before starting work. <Ref. to

NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.

3) Remove the push button ignition switch.
(1) Disconnect the connector, and remove the aspirator hose.
(2) Release the claws, and then remove the panel - switch.
(3) Release the claws, and then remove the push button ignition switch from the panel - switch.

B: INSTALLATION
1. MODEL WITHOUT KEYLESS ACCESS
1) Install the immobilizer antenna assembly.
2) Install the cover assembly - column.
3) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
2. MODEL WITH KEYLESS ACCESS
1) Install the push button ignition switch to the cover assembly - instrument panel LWR driver INN.
2) Install the cover assembly - instrument panel LWR driver.
3) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
