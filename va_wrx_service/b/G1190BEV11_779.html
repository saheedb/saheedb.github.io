LI-48
Side Turn Signal Light Assembly
LIGHTING SYSTEM
24.Side Turn Signal Light Assembly
A: REMOVAL
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the outer mirror assembly. <Ref. to GW-26, REMOVAL, Outer Mirror Assembly.>
3) Remove the side turn signal light assembly. <Ref. to GW-27, DISASSEMBLY, Outer Mirror Assembly.>
B: INSTALLATION
1) Install the side turn signal light assembly.
2) Install the outer mirror assembly.
Tightening torque:
7.5 N·m (0.8 kgf-m, 5.5 ft-lb)
3) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
