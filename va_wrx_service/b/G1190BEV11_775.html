Clearance/Parking Light Bulb
LIGHTING SYSTEM
20.Clearance/Parking Light Bulb
A: REMOVAL
NOTE:
Since the LED type is a non-disassembled part, it cannot be removed.
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Turn the steering wheel in the opposite direction from the parts to be removed. Then remove the clips and
turn over the mud guard - front.
3) Remove the parking light bulb. <Ref. to LI-25, PARKING LIGHT BULB, DISASSEMBLY, Headlight As-
sembly.>
B: INSTALLATION
1) Install the parking light bulb. <Ref. to LI-26, PARKING LIGHT BULB, ASSEMBLY, Headlight Assembly.>
2) Install the mud guard - front.
3) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
