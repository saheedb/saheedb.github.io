Trunk Lid Garnish
EXTERIOR/INTERIOR TRIM
13.Trunk Lid Garnish
A: REMOVAL
1) Disconnect the ground cable from battery. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, disconnect the ground terminal from battery sensor.
2) Remove the trim panel - trunk lid. <Ref. to EI-95, REMOVAL, Trunk Room Trim.>
3) Remove the garnish - trunk.
(1) Remove the nut.
(2) Disconnect the clips and connectors, and remove the garnish - trunk.
B: INSTALLATION
CAUTION:
Make sure that there is a gasket attached on the clip of the garnish assembly. If a gasket is not at-
tached, water leakage may occur.
1) Install the garnish - trunk.
Tightening torque:
4.5 N·m (0.5 kgf-m, 3.3 ft-lb)
2) Install the trim panel - trunk lid.
3) Connect the battery ground terminal. <Ref. to NT-5, BATTERY, NOTE, Note.>
NOTE:
For models other than STI model, connect the ground terminal to battery sensor.
